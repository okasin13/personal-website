Featured Song of the week archive

Here is an archive of all the featured songs on my homepage. Each week I pick a new song entry that I enjoy and want to share with others. Feel free to explore the list and discover some new music!

2026-02-11
Defttones first album, Adrenaline is often overlooked and considered one of their weaker albums, with a rougher, less polished sound compared to their later work. When compared to their more recent releases, you can quite literally see the difference in aggression and raw energy. Someone who has only listened to their later works may find that sound too different and uncomfortable, but on Engine No. 9, I'd say Chino screaming and rapping about drugs and skating is awesome. Engine No. 9 is a hidden gem amongst Deftones star-studded discography.
2026-02-04
This week’s pick is a little double feature: “Lovers of Artists” and “Take Pills” by Yerin Baek from her latest album, Flash and Core. Yes, I know — another Yerin Baek pick. But I’ve genuinely had these on repeat all week, and I swear I’ll spotlight more artists soon. Much like IU and Taeyeon, Yerin Baek is celebrated for her powerful vocals and an impressively versatile discography, and this album really puts both on full display. One of the best parts about Yerin Baek? She alternates between full Korean and English album releases, so there’s always something for international listeners to latch onto too!
2026-01-28
This song is coming on 10 years old now, where has the time gone? Yerin Baek has such a beautiful voice and this song really showcases her talent. Fun fact - this was an unreleased track that was only performed live until she released it on her 2019 album "Every Letter I Sent You".
